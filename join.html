Get involved / Join us

Join Us

Want to start projects or make contributions more conveniently? Join us!

Katorly Lab is looking for developers who are inquisitive, innovative and persevere, and wanting to make contributions to the world together.

Willing to contribute but tired of opening issues and pull requests? Wanting to start a project but have no teams to work together with?
Joining Katorly Lab may resolve troubles like this!

What you will gain

By becoming a member of Katorly Lab, you will acquire:

  1. Team support
  2. Access, clone and pull all existing repositories (including private)
  3. Direct push permission to specific repositories (specific requests needed)
  4. Create new repositories where you can start your project individually or with the team
  5. Start a business on your own
  6. and more...

How to join

To join Katorly Lab, make sure you have met at least one of the requirements below:

  • You are one of the maintainers or core contributers of a project in Katorly Lab.
  • You have positively contributed to Katorly Lab for 15 times.
  • You are invited as an honoured guest to Katorly Lab.
  • Prove your value to be a member of Katorly Lab.
  • Other requirements are subject to actual conditions.

Once you've make up your mind to join, request at HelpCenter/request, and follow the format below:

  • What you will gain
  • How to join
